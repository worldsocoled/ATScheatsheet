ATS Action Verbs Cheat Sheet

Your guide for using powerful verbs to showcase achievements

Article By: Nichole Higgins, 05/2025

Leadership & Management

  1. Led
  2. Managed
  3. Directed
  4. Supervised
  5. Mentored
  6. Orchestrated
  7. Coordinated
  8. Pioneered
  9. Championed
  10. Spearheaded
“Led a 12-person team to streamline workflows, cutting project delivery time by 25%.”

Technical & Hard Skills

  1. Developed
  2. Engineered
  3. Programmed
  4. Automated
  5. Debugged
  6. Optimized
  7. Integrated
  8. Architected
  9. Customized
  10. Validated
“Developed an AI-driven chatbot that reduced customer service inquiries by 40%.”

Communication & Collaboration

  2. Presented
  3. Authored
  4. Facilitated
  5. Liaised
  6. Mediated
  7. Influenced

  9. Translated
  10. Promoted
“Authored 50+ technical manuals adopted company-wide, reducing onboarding time by 30%.”

Innovation & Problem-Solving

  1. Designed
  2. Innovated
  3. Revamped
  4. Resolved
  5. Troubleshot
  6. Streamlined
  7. Transformed
  8. Overhauled
  9. Piloted
  10. Conceptualized
“Revamped a legacy CRM system, boosting sales team productivity by 35%.”

Analytical & Data-Driven

  1. Analyzed
  2. Forecasted
  3. Audited
  4. Quantified
  5. Modeled
  6. Evaluated
  7. Benchmarked
  8. Measured
  9. Interpreted
  10. Predicted
“Analyzed customer churn data to identify trends, reducing attrition by 22% in Q3.”

AI-Specific Verbs

  1. Automated
  2. Virtualized
  3. Scaled
  4. Leveraged (e.g., “Leveraged NLP models to...”)
  5. Trained (e.g., “Trained ML algorithms to...”)

3 Pro Tips for Using These Verbs

  1. Match the Job Description: Pull verbs directly from the job post
  2. Quantify Results: Always pair verbs with metrics
  3. Avoid Repetition: Use varied verbs for dynamic resumes
